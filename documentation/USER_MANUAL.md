## ðŸ‘¥ User Roles

### 1. Alumni Users
**Access**: Public registration and alumni dashboard

#### Registration Process
1. **Visit Registration Page**
   - Go to the alumni portal homepage
   - Click "Join Alumni Network"

2. **Choose Registration Method**
   - **SIS Verification (2014+ graduates)**
     - Enter student ID, date of birth, and graduation year
     - System automatically verifies with SIS
     - Complete contact information
     - Instant verification

   - **Manual Registration (Pre-2014 graduates)**
     - Fill personal and academic information
     - Upload proof document (certificate/transcript/ID)
     - Submit for admin verification
     - Approval within 2-3 business days

3. **Account Activation**
   - Verify email address
   - Set up password
   - Access alumni dashboard

#### Alumni Dashboard Features

**Profile Management**
- Update personal information
- Upload profile photo
- Manage professional details
- Control directory visibility

**Business Directory**
- Add your business listing
- Manage existing listings
- Search other alumni businesses
- Network with business owners

**Events**
- Browse upcoming events
- Register for events
- View registration history
- Receive event reminders

**Announcements**
- Read alumni news
- View job opportunities
- Access scholarships
- Read alumni success stories

## â“ Frequently Asked Questions

### Registration Issues
**Q: I can't remember my student ID**
A: Contact the alumni office for assistance with student ID recovery.

**Q: My documents are rejected**
A: Ensure documents are clear, legible, and show your name and graduation details.

**Q: I didn't receive verification email**
A: Check spam folder and ensure you entered the correct email address.

### Account Management
**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Contact admin for email changes as it affects verification.

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact the alumni office for account deletion requests.

### Business Directory
**Q: How long does business verification take?**
A: Typically 1-2 business days for initial review.

**Q: Can I feature my business?**
A: Featured spots are limited. Contact admin for featuring requests.

**Q: How do I update my business information?**
A: Go to "My Businesses" and click "Edit" on the relevant listing.
